Inspiration Website: Red Bull

I chose this website because it is both sophisticated yet vibrant and bold. This is how I would describe myself. Serious, going to get the job done well, but still bold and fun. As I go through this website every little aspect seems to pop without being too overwhelming. There are crazy and bright images on every page but they never seem to be in the way of the content that they are trying to get across.
